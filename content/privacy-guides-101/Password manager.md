---
title: Password Managers
---

Stop using "Password123". Use a password manager to generate and store unique, strong passwords for every site.

## Cloud-Based (Syncs across devices)

### [Bitwarden](https://bitwarden.com/)
The best overall choice.
*   **Why use it**: Open source, audited, reliable, and has a great free plan.

### [Proton Pass](https://proton.me/pass)
Newer entrant from Proton.
*   **Why use it**: Integrated with the Proton ecosystem using your existing account.

## Local-Based (Files stored on your device)

### [KeePassXC](https://keepassxc.org/)
The community fork of KeePass.
*   **How it works**: Uses an encrypted `.kdbx` file that you control. Store it on your PC, USB, or sync it yourself.

### [KeePassDX](https://www.keepassdx.com/) / [Keepass2Android](https://github.com/PhilippC/keepass2android)
Android clients for KeePass.
*   **Usage**: Open your `.kdbx` file on your phone.

> [!WARNING]
> Do NOT use browser built-in password managers. They lack the security features and portability of dedicated managers.
