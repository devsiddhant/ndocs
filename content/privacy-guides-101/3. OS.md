The foundation of your digital privacy.

## Mobile

### [GrapheneOS](https://grapheneos.org/)
The single most secure mobile OS. Pixel devices only.
*   **Features**: Hardened Android. De-Googled by default but has Sandbox Play Services for full app compatibility.

### [DivestOS](https://divestos.org/)
A soft fork of LineageOS with enhanced privacy and security.
*   **Best for**: Older devices that don't support GrapheneOS.

## Desktop

### Linux
See our **[Linux Section](/linux/recommended-distros)**. Moving away from Windows/macOS telemetry is the biggest step you can take.

### [Qubes OS](https://www.qubes-os.org/)
A reasonably secure operating system.
*   **Best for**: Edward Snowden level threat models.

### [Tails](https://tails.boum.org/)
Amnesic Incognito Live System.
*   **Best for**: Anonymous browsing from any computer.
