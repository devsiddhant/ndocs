---
title: 2FA / Authenticator
---

Two-Factor Authentication adds an extra layer of security effectively blocking 99.9% of account hack attempts.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Avoid SMS 2FA (SIM swapping risk) and Email 2FA (if email is compromised, everything is). Use TOTP (Time-based One-Time Password) apps.

## Recommended Apps

### [2FAS](https://2fas.com/)
Open source, offline, simple.
*   **Best for**: Most users. Has a browser extension to autofill codes.

### [Aegis Authenticator](https://getaegis.app/)
The gold standard for Android.
*   **Features**: Powerful encryption, biometric unlock, and flexible content backup options.

### [Ente Auth](https://ente.io/auth/)
Cross-platform (Mobile + Desktop).
*   **Best for**: Users who want end-to-end encrypted cloud backup to sync across devices.

### [Bitwarden](https://bitwarden.com)
Password manager with built-in 2FA.
*   **Best for**: Convenience. It copies the TOTP code to your clipboard automatically after autofilling your password.
