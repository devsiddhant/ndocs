---
title: Apps to Avoid
---

Popular software that is bad for your privacy.

| Category | Avoid ❌ | Use Instead ✅ | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Browser** | Google Chrome | **[Firefox](/windows/foss-apps#firefox)**, **[Brave](/windows/foss-apps#brave)** | Tracking, Manifest V3 (weakens adblockers). |
| **Search** | Google | **DuckDuckGo**, **SearXNG** | Tracking, filter bubbles. |
| **Messaging** | WhatsApp, Messenger | **Signal**, **SimpleX** | Metadata collection, owned by Meta. |
| **Email** | Gmail, Yahoo | **Proton Mail**, **Tutanota** | Scanning emails for data/ads. |
| **Passwords** | LastPass | **[Bitwarden](/privacy-guides-101/password-manager)** | History of major security breaches. |
| **Antivirus** | McAfee, Norton, Avast | **Windows Defender** | Bloatware, data collection, scareware tactics. |
| **Keyboard** | Gboard, SwiftKey | **FlorisBoard**, **OpenBoard** | Keylogging usage data. |
