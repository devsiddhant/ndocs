Reliable sources for torrents.

> [!CAUTION]
> Use an Adblocker (uBlock Origin) when visiting these sites. They are often filled with malicious ads.

## General

### [1337x](https://1337x.to/)
Great general purpose tracker.
*   **Best for**: Movies, TV, Games, Software. Good community moderation.

### [TorrentGalaxy](https://torrentgalaxy.to/)
Good for movies and TV shows.
*   **Best for**: Recent movie releases and camrips.

### [MagnetDL](https://www.magnetdl.com/)
Fast magnet link search engine.
*   **Best for**: Quickly finding a magnet link without a lot of UI clutter.

## Anime

### [Nyaa.si](https://nyaa.si/)
The ultimate source for Anime.
*   **Best for**: Everything anime (raws, subs, lossless). The holy grail.

### [Erai-raws](https://www.erai-raws.info/) / [SubsPlease](https://subsplease.org/)
Good release groups for currently airing shows.
*   **Best for**: Getting the latest seasonal anime episodes as soon as they air.

## Books / Audiobooks

### [Anna's Archive](https://annas-archive.org/)
The largest shadow library of books.
*   **Best for**: Finding literally any book, paper, or magazine. Aggregates LibGen and Z-Library.

### [LibGen](https://libgen.rs/)
Library Genesis.
*   **Best for**: Scientific papers, textbooks, and fiction.

### [AudioBookBay](https://audiobookbay.lu/)
Great for audiobooks.
