title: Getting a Free Domain
---

How to get a custom domain name for free.

## Providers

### [Eu.org](https://nic.eu.org/)
The most reliable free subdomain provider since 1996.
*   **Pros**: Looks like a top-level domain (your-name.eu.org).
*   **Cons**: Manual approval which can take weeks to months.

### [PP.ua](https://pp.ua/)
Free subdomains managed by NIC.UA.
*   **Pros**: Instant activation.
*   **Cons**: Requires phone verification (Telegram/SMS) and card verification (sometimes).

### [GitHub Student Developer Pack](https://education.github.com/pack)
If you are a student, apply for this pack.
*   **Benefits**: 1 free `.me` domain from Namecheap for a year, free hosting credits, and much more.

## Recommendation
If you are serious about your website, **buy a domain**. `.com` or `.org` domains are reliable and professional. Cloudflare Registrar sells them at cost (no markup).
