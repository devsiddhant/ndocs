title: Adobe Stack Alternatives
---

Free and Open Source alternatives to the Adobe Creative Cloud suite.

## Image Editing (Photoshop)

### [GIMP](https://www.gimp.org/)
The GNU Image Manipulation Program.
*   **Features**: Powerful photo retouching, image composition and image authoring. The closest FOSS equivalent to Photoshop.

### [Krita](https://krita.org/en/)
Professional free and open source painting program.
*   **Best for**: Digital painting and illustration.

## Vector Graphics (Illustrator)

### [Inkscape](https://inkscape.org/)
Professional quality vector graphics software.
*   **Features**: Flexible drawing tools, broad file format support, powerful text tools, and bezier curves.

## Video Editing (Premiere Pro)

### [DaVinci Resolve](https://www.blackmagicdesign.com/products/davinciresolve)
Professional editing, color correction, visual effects and audio post production.
*   **Note**: Proprietary (Closed Source), but the free version is industry standard and extremely powerful.

### [Kdenlive](https://kdenlive.org/en/)
An acronym for KDE Non-Linear Video Editor.
*   **Features**: Best fully Open Source video editor. Multi-track editing, proxy editing, and wide format support.

### [Shotcut](https://www.shotcut.org/)
A free, open source, cross-platform video editor.

## Photo Management (Lightroom)

### [Darktable](https://www.darktable.org/)
An open source photography workflow application and raw developer.
*   **Features**: Non-destructive editing for managing and developing raw images.

## Audio Editing (Audition)

### [Audacity](https://www.audacityteam.org/)
Free, open source, cross-platform audio software.
*   **Features**: Multi-track recording and editing.
