Modifications for popular apps to improve privacy or unlock features.

## Discord

### [Vencord](https://vencord.dev/)
A lightweight Discord client modification.
*   **Features**: Privacy-friendly, plugin support, and custom themes. Works in the browser or via a patched client.

### [Vesktop](https://github.com/Vencord/Vesktop)
A custom Discord App aiming to give better performance.
*   **Why use it**: The best standalone Discord client available.

## Spotify

### [Spicetify](https://spicetify.app/)
Command-line tool to customize the official Spotify client.
*   **Features**: Supports custom themes, extensions (like Adblock), and custom apps.

### [SpotX](https://github.com/SpotX-Official/SpotX)
Modified Spotify client for Windows.
*   **Features**: Blocks all audio, banner, and video ads.

## General Sources

### [Mobilism](https://forum.mobilism.org/)
The go-to forum for everything modded.
*   **Why use it**: Excellent source for Windows software cracks and Android mods.
*   *Warning*: Always check the comments and scan files with VirusTotal.

## Youtube

### [FreeTube](https://freetubeapp.io/)
An open source desktop YouTube player.
*   **Features**: Private YouTube client. No ads, no tracking, no Google account needed.
