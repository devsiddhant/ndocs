title: Bots
---

Useful bots to enhance your Telegram experience.

### [@Stickers](https://t.me/stickers)
The official bot to create sticker packs.
*   **Usage**: Send it images and emojis to start creating your own custom sticker sets.

### [@GmailBot](https://t.me/GmailBot)
Official Gmail bot.
*   **Usage**: Get notifications and reply to emails directly from Telegram.

### [@FileConverterBot](https://t.me/FileConverterBot)
Converts files from one format to another.
*   **Usage**: Send a file (image, audio, video) and choose the target format.

### [@DrWebBot](https://t.me/DrWebBot)
Virus checker.
*   **Usage**: Forward files or links to this bot to scan them for malware.

### [@DropMailBot](https://t.me/DropMailBot)
Generate disposable email addresses.
*   **Usage**: Quickly get a temp email for signups you don't trust.
