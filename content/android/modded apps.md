Apps modified to unlock features or improve privacy.

### [Morphe Manager](https://morphe.software/)
Make youtube and youtube music enjoyable
*   **What it does**: Patches official apps (YouTube, Reddit, Youtube music) to remove ads and add features.
*   *Note*: You need to provide your own APKs.

### [Mobilism](https://forum.mobilism.org/)
The legendary forum for modded Android apps.
*   **Why use it**: It is the most reliable source for cracked/modded APKs. It has a strict community and moderation.
*   *Tip*: Use an adblocker when visiting.

### [Vendetta / Vesktop](https://github.com/VendettaMod/Vendetta)
Modifications for Discord on Android.
*   **Features**: Plugins, themes, and robust privacy tools.

### [Pre-built Morphe apps](https://github.com/devsiddhant/Patched-apps)
These apps are built using github actions with APKs being retrieved from apkmirror. It is the same as patching on your device but just more convenient.
